Yoga classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
Intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga, this 60 minute class of poses and slow movment focuses on asana(proper alignment and posture), pranayama(breath work), and guided meditation to foster your mind and body connection.
Vinyasa Yoga
Although designed for intermediate to advanced students, beginners are welcome to sample this 60 minute class that focuses on breath-synchtonized movement-you will inhale ans exhale as you flow energetically through yoga poses.
Restorative Yoga
This 90 minute class features very slow movment and long poses that are supported by a chair or wall. This claming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experience. The practice can be a perfect Way to help rehabiliate an injury.
